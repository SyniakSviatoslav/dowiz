Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
Open the order status screen from your order history or tap the active order notification. You'll see live updates from preparation to delivery.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept cash (ALL) on delivery, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and mobile payments. Cash is the most popular in Tirana.
Can I change or cancel my order?
You can cancel within 2 minutes of placing the order. After that, please contact the restaurant directly.
What is the delivery zone?
We deliver within a 5 km radius of each restaurant. Enter your address to check if you're within the delivery zone.
How do I apply a promo code?
Enter your promo code in the cart screen before checkout. Promo codes cannot be combined.
