## 0. The one-paragraph verdict

The free, cash-only, relay-only pilot has **no identified legal blocker to order #1** — the repo's
G11 §5 finding survives adversarial re-verification. The venue is the merchant and fiscalizes on
its own POS (Law 87/2019 binds the invoice-issuing seller, not the order relay); dowiz charges
nothing, so there is no dowiz sale to register, fiscalize, or tax; Albania's new data-protection
law (124/2024) **abolished** the old register-with-the-Commissioner requirement, so a template
privacy notice + consent-at-order is a legitimate DIY posture at pilot scale; and couriers belong
to the venue, so the gig-classification exposure that defines aggregator legal risk simply does
not attach. The genuinely hard walls are all downstream: **charging the first lek** (entity +
dowiz's own fiscalization — accountant-serviceable), and **touching payments, employment, or
equity** (lawyer territory). Entity registration itself is a ~1-day, ~free, e-Albania self-service
act that accountants do routinely — it blocks *charging*, not *validating*.

## 1. Business entity — needed or not for a free cash pilot?

### 1.1 What the registration duty actually attaches to

- Albanian law requires QKB (National Business Center) registration for **any independent
  commercial activity**, with "no legal exemption for small-scale sellers or part-time operators"
  ([sherbimekontabiliteti e-commerce guide](https://sherbimekontabiliteti.al/en/ecommerce-online-business-tax-albania/)). **VERIFIED**
- One accountancy states the obligation begins "the moment **continuous economic activity**
  starts, regardless of the income level"
  ([AlProfit freelancer-NIPT guide](https://alprofitconsult.al/en/freelancers-must-register-as-individuals-with-a-tax-identification-number-tax-implications-risks-and-deadlines-until-2029/)). **VERIFIED (that the claim is made)**
- **The interpretive crux (ESTIMATE):** every enforcement mechanism found is keyed to *income and
  invoices* — the unregistered-activity penalty is ALL 50,000 (~€500) **plus back taxes on income
  earned while unregistered**; the unregistered-freelancer sanction is 15% tax **on gross
  income**; VAT attaches to "supplies **for consideration**"
  ([sherbimekontabiliteti](https://sherbimekontabiliteti.al/en/ecommerce-online-business-tax-albania/),
  [PwC tax summaries](https://taxsummaries.pwc.com/albania/corporate/other-taxes)). A pilot that
  charges **zero** produces no supply for consideration, no invoice, no income — nothing for
  those mechanisms to bite on. Giving a venue free software is, economically, what every
  open-source author does. The exposure of running the free pilot as an unregistered individual
  is therefore **plausibly nil in practice but not textually zero** — the conservative reading of
  "continuous economic activity" could cover a pre-revenue startup pilot.
- **Strongest framing (ESTIMATE):** *the venue uses a free tool.* The only business transacting is
  the venue (already registered, already fiscalizing). dowiz-the-individual is a toolmaker, not a
  trader, until the first lek is charged.

### 1.2 If/when you do register — cost, time, DIY-ability (no lawyer)

| Path | Cost | Time | Residency needed? | DIY? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Person fizik (sole trader)** | Free online via e-Albania (ALL 100 in person) | ~1 business day; NIPT issued automatically | **Yes** — foreigners need an Albanian biometric residence permit + AL phone number to hold the e-Albania account that files the application | Fully self-service ([Deel](https://www.deel.com/blog/sole-proprietorship-albania/), [AlProfit](https://alprofitconsult.al/en/business-registration-2/sole-proprietor/initial-registration-of-a-natural-person/), [Santi & Partners](https://santi-partners.com/2026/04/27/how-to-register-business-albania-foreigners/), [sherbimekontabiliteti e-Albania guide](https://sherbimekontabiliteti.al/en/albania-e-albania-portal-guide/)) **VERIFIED** |
| **SHPK (LLC)** | Registration free; minimum capital **ALL 100** (~€1) | 1–2 business days via QKB | **No** — 100% foreign ownership, no resident shareholder/director required | DIY if you can file; foreign-language founding docs need Albanian translation + **notarization**; remote founding via notarized PoA (a notary/accountant task, not a lawyer's) ([Oracle Law](https://oraclelawglobal.com/news/company-formation-in-albania-setting-up-an-llc-shpk/), [Consul.al](https://consul.al/en/albania-llc/), [Commenda](https://www.commenda.io/albania/business-setup), [A-Law](https://www.a-law.eu/en/legal-advice/setting-company-albania)) **VERIFIED** |

- Accountants and consultancies advertise and perform these registrations routinely — the sources
  above *are* accountancies. **VERIFIED.** Registration is an administrative filing, not legal work.
- **Tax cost of registering early is near zero:** 0% profit tax until **Dec 31, 2029** for any
  sole trader/self-employed/entity with turnover ≤ **ALL 14M** (~€140K), conditional on (1) NIPT
  registration, (2) a **business bank account** (required within ~20 days of registration), (3)
  staying under the threshold ([AlProfit 2026 rules](https://alprofitconsult.al/en/rules-2026-for-foreigners-in-albania/),
  [AlProfit freelancer guide](https://alprofitconsult.al/en/freelancers-must-register-as-individuals-with-a-tax-identification-number-tax-implications-risks-and-deadlines-until-2029/)). **VERIFIED.**
  VAT registration only above **ALL 10M** turnover/12 months ([PwC](https://taxsummaries.pwc.com/albania/corporate/other-taxes), [tatime.gov.al](https://www.tatime.gov.al/eng/c/4/96/110/value-added-tax)). **VERIFIED**
- Ongoing cost once registered: small-business accounting from **~ALL 5,000/month**
  ([Albania Ekonomist](https://albaniaekonomist.com/en/services/accounting/)). **VERIFIED (one provider's list price; treat as floor) / ESTIMATE for market range**

### 1.3 What stays blocked until an entity (NIPT) exists

- **Charging money** — a subscription invoice requires a NIPT and fiscalization of dowiz's *own*
  sale (see §2.3). This is the repo's correct "SHPK placeholder blocks CHARGING not VALIDATING"
  finding. **VERIFIED**
- **Issuing any invoice** (fiscal or e-invoice). **VERIFIED**
- **Commercial contracts in a trade name** — an individual can validly sign a simple free-pilot
  letter of understanding under general freedom of contract (**ESTIMATE** — general civil-law
  principle, not Albania-source-verified), but a paid B2B service contract + invoicing needs the
  registered entity behind it.
- **Business bank account, hiring, VAT credit.** **VERIFIED**
- Under the E-commerce Law 10128/2009, "information society services" (defined as services
  provided at a distance, electronically, **against compensation**) may be provided by persons
  **registered with the National Registration Centre** without special authorization — i.e., the
  moment dowiz becomes a *paid* online service it must be a registered provider and display
  minimum identifying info (name, seat, email, registration data) on the site
  ([Stanford WILMap](https://wilmap.stanford.edu/entries/law-no-10128-electronic-commerce),
  [Lexology/Hoxha Memi Hoxha](https://internationallawoffice.com/Newsletters/Tech-Data-Telecoms-Media/Albania/Hoxha-Memi-Hoxha/E-commerce-and-contracts-concluded-by-electronic-means)). A *free* service
  arguably falls outside the "against compensation" definition entirely. **VERIFIED (law's terms) / ESTIMATE (free-service reading)**

### 1.4 Verdict

**A registered entity is NOT required to run the free relay-only pilot** (ESTIMATE, moderate-high
confidence; conservative counter-reading exists), and the venue-uses-a-free-tool framing makes the
individual's exposure minimal. **Register before the first charged lek** — and given that
registration is ~free, ~1 day, self-service, and 0%-taxed until 2029, the cheap insurance move is
to register *whenever residence-permit logistics allow*, without letting it gate the pilot.
Person-fizik needs a residence permit first; SHPK does not (but needs notarized/translated docs).
Downside case if the conservative reading ever bit: **ALL 50,000** (~€500) + back taxes on income
— which is zero during a genuinely free pilot. **VERIFIED (fine amount)**

## 2. Fiscalization (Law 87/2019) — does dowiz trigger it? VERDICT: NO (relay-only, free)

### 2.1 Who the law binds

- Law 87/2019 ("On the invoice and circulation monitoring system") obliges **registered taxpayers
  who issue invoices** — the seller/merchant — plus public entities and "banks, financial
  institutions and other intermediaries **facilitating the payment** of electronic invoices"
  ([SNI](https://snitechnology.net/new-fiscalization-regulation-in-albania/),
  [Mondaq/KPMG](https://www.mondaq.com/tax-authorities/1059296/new-fiscalization-tax-reform-requires-e-invoicing),
  [sherbimekontabiliteti fiskalizimi guide](https://sherbimekontabiliteti.al/en/fiskalizimi-albania/)). **VERIFIED**
- The obligation sits with the party issuing the invoice: "the **seller must fiscalize at
  issuance**" ([sherbimekontabiliteti](https://sherbimekontabiliteti.al/en/fiskalizimi-albania/)). **VERIFIED**
- COD/e-commerce mechanics: invoice issued **at the moment of sale**, NIVF generated in ~2s;
  electronic delivery of the invoice to the buyer is sufficient for online orders (no print
  needed) ([sherbimekontabiliteti](https://sherbimekontabiliteti.al/en/fiskalizimi-albania/),
  corroborates repo R4/gap-analysis). **VERIFIED**

### 2.2 The dowiz posture

In the pilot: the **venue** is the merchant of record, issues the fiscal receipt (NIVF/QR) on its
**own existing certified POS**, cash settles customer→venue directly, dowiz transmits order data
only and charges nothing. dowiz issues **no invoice**, generates **no NIVF**, and **facilitates no
payment**. None of the law's bound categories describes it. **VERDICT: no fiscalization
obligation attaches to dowiz for the free relay-only pilot.** **VERIFIED (who is bound) +
ESTIMATE (application to dowiz — no source addresses order-relay platforms explicitly; the
[sherbimekontabiliteti guide](https://sherbimekontabiliteti.al/en/fiskalizimi-albania/) confirms no
exemption *or* obligation is stated for non-paying, non-invoicing intermediary platforms).**
Accountant sign-off on the relay regime (repo MAX-ROI #3) remains the cheap belt-and-braces —
before charging subscriptions, not before order #1.

- Note: **no Albanian DAC7-analogue found** — no platform-operator reporting obligation for
  marketplaces (DAC7 is an EU directive; Albania is a candidate, not a member; searches surfaced
  no Albanian implementation). **UNVERIFIED (absence of law — checked, none found July 2026)**

### 2.3 The exact trigger moments (flag these on the roadmap)

1. **dowiz charges its first subscription** → dowiz's *own* sale must be fiscalized: NIPT +
   certified fiscal software from the tax authority's list ("any software not on that list cannot
   legally generate NIVF codes") ([sherbimekontabiliteti](https://sherbimekontabiliteti.al/en/fiskalizimi-albania/)). **VERIFIED.**
   Serviceable by an accountant + an off-the-shelf certified solution (e.g.
   [easypos.al](https://easypos.al/blog/cfare-eshte-fiskalizimi-ne-shqiperi), [oxana.al](https://oxana.al/fiscalization-software-albania/?lang=en)); no lawyer needed.
2. **dowiz issues receipts/e-invoices on behalf of venues or generates NIVF** (closing repo
   blocker G1 for scale) → dowiz's software must be certified / from a **registered software
   producer-maintainer** — Albania keeps a register of fiscalization-software producers and
   maintainers with specific requirements ([Fiscal Solutions](https://www.fiscal-requirements.com/news/725);
   detailed scope paywalled — **UNVERIFIED in detail**, but by the system's definition it covers
   invoice-issuing software, which relay-only dowiz is not).
3. **dowiz ever processes prepaid/online payment** → it enters the payment chain the law names
   ("intermediaries facilitating payment") **and** raises payment-services/e-money licensing
   questions (Bank of Albania) — this is a **hard lawyer trigger** (§5.2). **VERIFIED (category
   exists in law) / ESTIMATE (licensing consequence)**

### 2.4 Climate check (why "never add fiscal risk" stands)

2026 enforcement: penalties auto-generated within 24h of missed declarations; non-fiscalization
fines tripled for legal persons; POS-terminal mandate hits **all remaining taxpayers Dec 31,
2026** (tourism/transport already bound since May 30, 2026); non-compliant-invoice fines ALL
50,000 (natural persons) to ALL 500,000 (repeat, legal persons)
([repo §2.2, primary-sourced](/root/dowiz/docs/research/2026-07-11-adoption-ev-market.md),
[sherbimekontabiliteti](https://sherbimekontabiliteti.al/en/fiskalizimi-albania/),
[JB Fiscal](https://jbfiscalconsulting.com/albania-fiscalization-implementation-schedule-and-penalties/)). **VERIFIED.**
dowiz must keep the storefront flow *ending* in the venue's own fiscal receipt — the pilot's
relay-only design already does.

## 3. Data protection — minimal DIY-compliant posture

### 3.1 Current law (repo's framing confirmed current)

- **Law 124/2024 "On Personal Data Protection"** replaced Law 9887/2008: passed 19 Dec 2024,
  Official Gazette 17 Jan 2025, **in force 31 Jan/Feb 2025**; fully GDPR-aligned
  ([IAPP](https://iapp.org/news/a/albania-s-personal-data-protection-law-a-legal-framework-harmonized-with-the-gdpr),
  [KPMG](https://kpmg.com/al/en/insights/2025/02/new-law-on--personal-data-protection-.html),
  [DLA Piper](https://www.dlapiperdataprotection.com/?t=law&c=AL),
  [official English text (idp.al)](https://idp.al/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Law-no.124-2024-DP.pdf)). **VERIFIED.**
  The repo's `compliance/` 124/2024 framing is correct and current.
- Enforcement is ramping: 6 fines in the first two months of 2026 vs 2 in all of the prior year
  ([EY Albania](https://www.ey.com/en_al/insights/law/albania-data-protection-commissioner-under-the-new-data-protection-law)). **VERIFIED**
- Fines: up to **ALL 1bn or 2% of global turnover** (standard) / **ALL 2bn or 4%** (severe),
  whichever is higher ([KPMG](https://kpmg.com/al/en/insights/2025/02/new-law-on--personal-data-protection-.html),
  concordant with [DLA Piper](https://www.dlapiperdataprotection.com/?t=law&c=AL); one secondary
  source ([Alba Legal](https://albalegal.eu/?p=1058)) cites lower figures — the KPMG/DLA reading
  is taken as authoritative). **VERIFIED (with noted discrepancy)**

### 3.2 The DIY wins — what is NOT required for this pilot

- **No registration/notification of processing with the Commissioner** — the old general
  notification obligation was **abolished** by 124/2024
  ([CMS Expert Guide](https://cms.law/en/int/expert-guides/cms-expert-guide-to-data-protection-and-cyber-security-laws/albania)). **VERIFIED.** (Under old Law 9887 this was a real filing; it's gone.)
- **No DPO** — mandatory only for public authorities, large-scale regular/systematic monitoring,
  or sensitive/criminal data at scale ([DLA Piper](https://www.dlapiperdataprotection.com/index.html?t=data-protection-officers&c=AL),
  [Clym](https://www.clym.io/regulations/law-no-1242024-on-personal-data-protection-albania)).
  A Durrës pilot processing name/phone/address/order data for a handful of venues hits none of
  these. **VERIFIED (thresholds) / ESTIMATE (pilot below them — clearly so)**
- **No SCCs for EU hosting** — Commissioner **Decision No. 1 (April 2025)** deems EU/EEA,
  Convention-108 countries, and EC-adequacy countries adequate; transfers there need no
  additional safeguards ([Karanovic & Partners](https://www.karanovicpartners.com/news/albania-aligns-with-gdpr-new-decision-on-adequate-countries-for-data-transfers/)). **VERIFIED.**
  → Action: pin Fly.io region to an EU region and record it in `compliance/subprocessors.md`;
  US-region hosting would lean on the EU–US DPF adequacy chain (workable but messier — **ESTIMATE**).

### 3.3 The minimal compliant posture (all template-serviceable)

1. **Privacy notice** (sq + en) naming the controller, purposes, legal bases, retention, rights,
   contact — self-authored from reputable GDPR templates; 124/2024 mirrors GDPR Art. 13/14 duties.
   **VERIFIED (duty) / ESTIMATE (template sufficiency at this scale — standard practice)**
2. **Consent + notice at order** — repo already has the pattern (`compliance/consent-records.md`:
   consent as DB rows with `consent_at` + `privacy_version`). Opt-in, no pre-checked boxes
   ([DLA Piper](https://www.dlapiperdataprotection.com/?t=law&c=AL)). Note: for order fulfillment
   itself the cleaner basis is *contractual necessity*; reserve consent for anything extra
   (marketing). **ESTIMATE (basis choice — standard GDPR analysis)**
3. **Data minimization** — name, phone, address, order contents only; no birthdate, no ID
   numbers, nothing sensitive. Under-16 online consent needs parental authorization (Art. 8(6))
   — moot if the flow doesn't target minors. **VERIFIED (rule)**
4. **Controller/processor mapping** — the **venue is controller** of its customers' data; dowiz is
   **processor** (or, in the ratified local-first end-state where data lives on the vendor's node
   and dowiz never accesses it, arguably a mere software supplier outside the
   controller/processor pair — a genuine structural advantage). Sign the DPA template already in
   `compliance/contracts/dpa-with-owners-template.md`. **Honesty check:** today's staging/prod is
   centrally hosted on Fly.io, so dowiz IS a processor holding data until local-first ships —
   the advantage is real but *prospective*. **ESTIMATE (role analysis; GDPR-standard)**
5. **Breach process** — 72h notification duty to the Commissioner (Art. 35) + documentation;
   a one-page runbook satisfies the *readiness* need at this scale
   ([DLA Piper](https://www.dlapiperdataprotection.com/?t=law&c=AL)). **VERIFIED (duty)**
6. **Fix the placeholder** — `compliance/README.md` controller = "[SHPK назва ____]". During the
   pre-entity pilot the factual controller-side identity is the operator personally (for dowiz's
   own site data) and each venue (for its customers). Name them; a blank controller is worse than
   a personal name. **ESTIMATE (housekeeping)**

### 3.4 What genuinely needs counsel (data-protection edition)

Nothing at pilot scale, per the thresholds above. Counsel becomes advisable when: processing
becomes **large-scale** (many venues, systematic profiling → DPIA and possibly DPO), you monetize
data or add marketing profiling, a **high-risk processing** authorization question arises
(specific Commissioner authorization survives for some high-risk cases —
[CMS](https://cms.law/en/int/expert-guides/cms-expert-guide-to-data-protection-and-cyber-security-laws/albania)),
or the Commissioner opens any inquiry. **VERIFIED (trigger categories) / ESTIMATE (timing)**

## 4. Liability surface — the own-couriers advantage

### 4.1 Gig-labor classification: sidestepped. VERDICT: YES

- **Albania has no platform-work / rider law** — searches surfaced none, and no draft
  (July 2026). The EU Platform Work Directive (2024/2831, presumption-of-employment machinery)
  binds **EU member states** (transposition Dec 2026); Albania is a candidate country and not
  bound — alignment will come with accession chapters, years out. **UNVERIFIED (absence of law —
  checked multiple angles, none found)**
- The live local tension is real but belongs to aggregators: Wolt couriers in Albania are
  **independent contractors**, and Tirana Wolt couriers ran a multi-day protest in Sept 2024 over
  fee cuts ([Europeum report](https://www.europeum.org/wp-content/uploads/Albania.pdf),
  [Wolt courier page](https://explore.wolt.com/en/alb/couriers)). **VERIFIED**
- dowiz's structure: couriers are the **venue's own staff** — ordinary Albanian Labour Code
  employment (or whatever arrangement the venue already has), contracted to and paid by the
  venue. dowiz has no contract with couriers, pays them nothing, does not recruit, schedule,
  dispatch-control, or rate them (the ratified **no-courier-scoring** decision is load-bearing
  here: every classification test worldwide keys on *control*, and scoring/algorithmic management
  is the classic control evidence). **The exposure that sank/pressured aggregators (misclassified
  fleets) does not attach to a party with no courier relationship at all.** **ESTIMATE
  (posture analysis — high confidence; no Albanian authority has ruled on the pattern)**
- **Control-creep tripwires** (any of these re-opens the question): dowiz assigning/routing
  couriers across venues, courier ratings/leaderboards, dowiz-branded courier gear, dowiz paying
  or bonusing couriers, a shared courier pool. Keep couriers venue-side in fact, not just on paper.

### 4.2 Food-safety and sale liability: sits with the venue. VERDICT: HOLDS, with hygiene

- The **venue is the food business operator and the seller**: the contract of sale is
  venue↔customer; consumer-protection duties for distance selling (Law 9902/2008 framework) fall
  on the trader ([Lexology/HMH](https://internationallawoffice.com/Newsletters/Tech-Data-Telecoms-Media/Albania/Hoxha-Memi-Hoxha/E-commerce-and-contracts-concluded-by-electronic-means),
  [CMS e-commerce guide](https://cms.law/en/int/expert-guides/ecommerce-in-cee/albania)). **VERIFIED (framework) / ESTIMATE (application)**
- Albania's E-commerce Law 10128/2009 imports the EU e-Commerce Directive's **intermediary
  liability shields** (mere conduit / hosting: no liability for third-party information where the
  provider doesn't initiate, modify, or select) — doctrinal support for "dowiz is the software
  layer" ([Stanford WILMap](https://wilmap.stanford.edu/entries/law-no-10128-electronic-commerce),
  [Boga & Associates briefing](http://www.bogalaw.com/library/e-commerce.html)). **VERIFIED**
- **ToS/LoU language that establishes the framing** (self-authorable, template-grade):
  (a) dowiz provides software only; it is not the seller, employer, courier operator, or payment
  agent; (b) the sale contract is exclusively between venue and customer; (c) the venue is solely
  responsible for menu accuracy, pricing, food safety, allergen info, fiscal receipt issuance,
  and delivery by its own personnel; (d) COD only — dowiz never holds funds; (e) storefront
  displays the **venue's** name and NIPT as the seller. **ESTIMATE (standard platform framing)**
- **Residual risks (named):** storefront presentation that confuses customers about who the
  seller is (mitigate: venue name/NIPT prominent); menu/price errors displayed by dowiz software
  (mitigate: venue-confirms-content clause); the accepted-risk re-hosted Google-sourced photos
  (repo, operator-accepted 2026-07-01 — an IP, not safety, exposure); a "free" pilot that
  behaves like a paid commercial relationship (mitigate: LoU states free, either side may stop,
  no exclusivity); and the pre-entity period leaving the operator personally behind the ToS
  (mitigate: register entity before scale — §1). **ESTIMATE**
