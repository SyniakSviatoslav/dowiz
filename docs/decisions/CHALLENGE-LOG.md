# Challenge Log — the `challenge` channel (§7)

> Governed by [../operating-model/agent-operating-model.md](../operating-model/agent-operating-model.md).
> How to question an authoritative decision/paradigm **without fear** and **without scope-creep**.

## Rules
- A challenge = a **logged challenge with evidence** here (label `challenge`), NOT an informal chat reopen.
- **Reopen is allowed only by NEW real-world evidence** (a pilot says cash reconciliation is
  unbearable; PostHog shows a drop at step X). Second-guessing / "what if" — no.
- A challenge that brings evidence is **welcomed as value**, not insubordination — this is
  psychological safety (§4) bounded by the decision registry (🔴).
- A challenge does not change execution on its own — it's an input, not a verdict. Resolution:
  real blind spot → spawn a `spike` or revise the decision; noise → close with a note.
